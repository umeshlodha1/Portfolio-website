ParkIn Mobile App

The problem of parking is rising day by day specially in Metropolitan cities like Mumbai, Pune, Delhi, Kolkata, Bangalore, Hyderabad, etc. It’s difficult to find a parking there is no predictability whether you may or not get parking before leaving the house. To Overcome this problem, we came up with ParkIn a parking Booking App that reserves parking for you in real time with its powerful AI and real time tracking. PARKIN helps you to find a car parking area near you and book it digitally from any where in advance. It provies Parking of electrical vehicle with easy charging facility provided in parking space. User can get estimated cost of charging booking its completely based on cashless transaction. Parkin provide smart and secured parking solution based on the intelligent RFID/QR Code technology. parking owner can easily monitor transactions and manage parking lots and employee.

Safetify Women Safety App

Safetify is a mobile app that helps to send alerts and notify your family and friends in case of emergencies. Features: SOS feature sends out an alert message to your trusted contacts with your current or last known location(in case of no internet) “Find Me” feature shares your live location with your trusted contacts. Additionally, you can send text messages, image and voice updates like taxi number plate while traveling. Locate and navigate Nearby Safe Places such as police stations, hospitals Learn feature allows you to learn self-defense techniques, each technique has step by step clear description and visuals Emergency helpline number to directly call from the app.

News Website

This news website is equipped with a comprehensive administrative control system, an editor panel, and an innovative news subscription feature. The admin control facilitates efficient management of the platform, ensuring seamless operations and content oversight. The editor panel allows for easy content creation, editing, and publication, empowering editorial teams to deliver timely and engaging news. One of the standout features is the News Subscriptions functionality, enabling users to stay updated effortlessly.
